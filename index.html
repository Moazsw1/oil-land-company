Wooden Pallets Production Project

The Wooden Pallets Production Project focuses on manufacturing high-quality pallets widely used in the food industry and various types of seating. Additionally, wooden pallets are utilized in building structures, and the outer layer of wood can be used for decorative purposes. These pallets are also employed in the production of compressed and thick wooden panels, which are used in household furniture manufacturing.
